How to Buy MoonCat on Moonshot

1
Install the Moonshot App

Download Moonshot from the App Store or Google Play. It's a self-custodial wallet with Fiat on-ramp via Apple Pay, cards, or bank transfers.

2
Create and Fund Your Wallet

Sign up with email and enable Face ID/PIN security. Add funds in USD or crypto (SOL, USDC) using Apple Pay, credit card, PayPal, or transfers.

3
Connect & Explore Tokens

Tap “Connect” or navigate to the discovery tab. Browse trending or new tokens. Search specifically for MoonCat (MC) by name.

4
Buy MoonCat

Open MoonCat’s token page in the app. Enter the USD or SOL amount you want to spend. Tap “Buy”, then approve the on-chain transaction.

5
Manage Your Holdings

MoonCat appears in your portfolio with live charts (price, volume, market cap). You can sell anytime by using the “Sell” button on the token page.
